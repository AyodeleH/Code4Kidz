Who Are We?

The Code4Kids mission is to motivate students, especially, students of color, to believe in and build upon their technological capabilities.

Our goal is to create a platform for schools in low income neighborhoods to have an accessible and affordable way to access technological resources like computers, laptops, tablets, coding activities for students, programming lessons for teachers, and much more!

Technology is like art, and we want to enhance the art which kids have within them, so each indivdual can realize the uniquness they hold and take initiative.

Code4Kids

No matter where you come from nor, your financial status, each and every child has the capacity to make a difference in this world, and in this ever-progressing Digital Age, what better way to do so than through the devices we use everyday!
